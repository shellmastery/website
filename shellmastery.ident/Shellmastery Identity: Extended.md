### 6. Copy Style Guide

*How to format, phrase, and flow content (e.g., emoji use, contractions, tone tips):*

* **Emojis**: Avoid on the website. Optional in articles and GitHub readmes if sparingly used for tone or clarity. Freely used on social media.
* **Contractions**: Use them freely wherever they make the writing feel natural and readable.
* **Tone**: Instructional and reference materials should remain professional yet light‚Äînot rigid or overly casual. All other content can use a casual, fun, or even slightly cheeky tone when appropriate.
* **Formatting**: Use backticks (`like this`) for commands and code snippets. Prefer monospace for inline commands and clear block formatting for longer examples.
